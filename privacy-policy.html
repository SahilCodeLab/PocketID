Your documents and ID data are stored using military-grade encryption. We do not store your sensitive documents on our servers; they are kept locally on your device or in your personal encrypted cloud backup (Google Drive/iCloud) which you control.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our application (like payment processors), as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
