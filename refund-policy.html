Last updated: April 21, 2026

Digital Goods Policy

PocketID Pro Membership and Digital Certificates are classified as digital goods. As per standard industry rules, once a digital certificate has been issued or premium access has been granted, no refunds will be provided.

1. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle. To cancel, please use the management options within the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, or contact us directly.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only considered in the following cases:

  • Duplicate payments made due to technical glitches.

  • Unintentional purchases where the digital goods have not been accessed or downloaded yet (subject to verification).

3. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and will be credited back to the original payment method used during the transaction.
